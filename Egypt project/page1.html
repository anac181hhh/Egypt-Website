Tourism

Tourism in Egypt is all about exploring its rich history, amazing landmarks, and beautiful landscapes. In 2013, Egypt ranked 85th as the world's best country in terms of tourism.


Cairo

Cairo is a great place to visit for many fun reasons! First, it's home to the amazing pyramids, which are huge, ancient structures built a long time ago as tombs for kings. You can actually see these giant buildings and even go inside some of them!

Next, Cairo has the Nile River, which is the longest river in the world. You can take boat rides on it and see the city from the water!

Cairo is also full of history and culture. There are museums where you can see mummies and treasures from ancient Egypt. The people in Cairo are friendly, and you can try delicious food, like falafel and kebabs.

Plus, there are bustling markets where you can shop for unique souvenirs, like handmade crafts and colorful clothes. Overall, Cairo is a place where you can learn, explore, and have a lot of fun!

Luxor

Luxor is an awesome place to visit for lots of exciting reasons! First, it's like a giant open-air museum filled with ancient Egyptian history. Here, you can see some of the coolest temples and tombs, like the famous Valley of the Kings, where many pharaohs were buried with treasures.

One of the most famous sites in Luxor is the Karnak Temple, which has huge columns and statues that make you feel like you’ve stepped back in time. It’s super impressive and makes for great photos!

Also, Luxor is right next to the Nile River, so you can take fun boat rides and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The sunsets over the river are amazing!

The people in Luxor are really welcoming, and you can try yummy local food too. Plus, there are lots of fun activities, like riding camels or exploring colorful markets. Overall, Luxor is a magical place where you can learn about ancient Egypt while having a great time!
